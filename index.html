What is unique about Black GSD'S

  • It is very rare as an offspring can be truly black only when one of the parents are black or better both!
  • It has an extremely dense and soft coat. It is mostly popular for it's shiny pitch black coat
  • The change in looks does not make it any different from the common Germie
